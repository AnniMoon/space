Uranus

Name: Uranus ♅

Diameter: approx. 50,724 km

Special Features:
Seventh planet from the Sun
Composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane
Has a pale blue color due to methane in its atmosphere
Rotates on its side with an extreme axial tilt of about 98°
